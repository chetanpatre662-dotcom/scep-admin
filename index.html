About the App

Built exclusively for SCEP students, faculty, and administrators to streamline campus transportation.

  • Real-time GPS tracking of all college buses
  • Digital bus pass generation and verification
  • Role-based access for students, drivers, and admins
  • Instant push notifications for schedule changes
  • In-app support chat for quick assistance
  • Secure Firebase authentication
