Account & Data Deletion

Thank you for using our Bus Tracking Application. We respect your privacy and provide a simple process for requesting the deletion of your account and personal data.

How to Request Account Deletion

If you wish to permanently delete your account and associated data, please follow these steps:

  • Open the Bus Tracking Application.
  • Log in to your account.
  • Go to the Support section.
  • Create a new requesting Account Deletion.
  • Our administrator will review your request.
  • Once verified, your account and personal information will be permanently deleted.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

  • User profile information
  • Login credentials
  • Bus pass information
  • Complaint history
  • Any other personal data linked to your account

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are generally processed within 7 business days after verification by the administrator.

Data Retention

Certain information may be retained for a limited period if required by applicable laws, legal obligations, fraud prevention, or security purposes. Any retained data will only be kept for the duration required by law.

Important:
Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Once your account has been deleted, you will lose access to all associated services and data.
